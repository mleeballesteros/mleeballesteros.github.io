About Me

Hi there! I'm Mark Lee, but feel free to call me Lee—web developer and a slightly frustrated content creator. My journey into web development started back in college when my thesis team needed someone to take charge of our website project. Even though I didn’t know much at the time, I mustered up all my courage, took on the challenge, and guess what? We ended up with a high grade! Whoohoo!

Did you know that coffee is basically fuel for quality coding? It's what keeps my projects—many of which are still running smoothly—alive! So, how about grabbing a coffee sometime? And speaking of coding, it's always funny how debugging—though frustrating at first—turns into a good laugh when you realize the issue was just a missing quotation mark or semicolon.

When I'm not coding, I love creating content, especially videos from my gaming sessions. If you're into that, feel free to check it out and maybe subscribe (no pressure, lol)!

I definitely wouldn't be where I am today without the support of my friends and mentors. I'm incredibly grateful to my seniors who guided me and to my friends who never got tired of my wild ideas. They've all played a huge role in shaping who I am today.
